Don't Get Distracted by the City

by Israel Shaw

I met Israel Shaw in a BBQ party in New York City through a mutual friend of mine, Anthony. Hours into conversation he showed me one of his poems, 'Don't Get Distracted by the City'. His poem got my attention, especially those two lines in which he writes insignificant of us in this vast universe: We fall in circles through a billion galaxies, hanging in a fragile orbit. Part of which connects to my previous post of Journey Wisely.
